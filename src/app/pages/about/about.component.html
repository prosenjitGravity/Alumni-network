Our Vision and Mission Statement

The mission of the Alumni Network is to open channels of communication between the college and the alumni and provide them with the optimum careers guidance . The Alumni Network is also keen to provide the graduates with the necessary training that will enable them to respond actively requirements. The Network sets up mechanisms to maintain contact between the alumni and the college on the one hand and the regional and local community institutions on the other hand.

Vision

NSEC Alumni Network shall serve as a platform to bridge the gap of student alumni interaction driven by the ideals and values that shall ensure the upliftment of both present and future alumnus with support to build a social, knowledgeable and motivational capital for Netaji Subhash Engineering College and its students.
